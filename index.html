Hi, I'm Elena Fernandez, a data specialist and a bioinformatician

My background in the world of data and genomics starts academically with bioinformatics and statistical analysis. My curiosity quickly led me to expand my knowledge in open source software development projects and collaborations in other research groups solving different problems. I have also applied my knowledge in professional environments through the use of business intelligence. My favourite programming languages are Python and SQL, although I also use R and tools such as Power BI. If I would like to highlight something it is my experience with the use of international databases in the biosciences area. Additionally, although it is not my area of expertise, I have started in the world of web development. I find it necessary to be able to give visibility to one's work and also to understand the purpose of your work within an organisation whose ultimate goal is some kind of application. In short, my experience includes activities in the academic and professional world as well as freelance work and personal projects.
